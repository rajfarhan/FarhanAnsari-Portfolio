Software Developer

A software developer is a person who specializes in computer programming. They are responsible for designing, creating and modifying programs to meet the needs of their employers.The role of a software developer has changed over time. It used to be that they would write code for the general public and make it available to download.

Internet

The internet is a global system of interconnected computer networks that use the standard Internet protocol suite (TCP/IP). It is a network of networks that consists of private, public, academic, business, and government networks of local to global scope that are linked by a broad array of electronic and optical technologies.
